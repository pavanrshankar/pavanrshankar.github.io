Pavan Ravishankar

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences - NYU, advised by Prof. Daniel Neill. I am a member of the ML4G lab. My PhD is fully funded by the MacCracken fellowship.

Previously, I completed my M.S (By Research) in Computer Science from IIT Madras, guided by Prof. Balaraman Ravindran and Prof. Sudarsan Padmanabhan, and B.S in Information Systems from BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus.

Research Interests: I am interested in Responsible Machine Learning (ML), specifically in end-to-end fairness in the ML pipeline.

If you're interested in working with me, please don't hesitate to get in touch!
