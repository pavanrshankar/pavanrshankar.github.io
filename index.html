Research Interests

I am broadly interested in Responsible AI, spanning both the systemic and societal aspects of using AI. On the systemic front, I am interested in developing fair machine learning (ML) algorithms. On the societal front, I am interested in evaluating the ramifications of using AI. My current focus is to understand how bias propagates across various stages of the ML pipeline.
